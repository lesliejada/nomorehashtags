Uplifting one another by celebrating achievement in our community

Congratulations to Michigan State's very own LaTeishia Miggins. She is a fall 2016 graduate receiving her Bachelors of Science in Criminal Justice. LaTeishia is a first generation college student and has a younger brother at the university as well. She plans to take her talents to Arizona where she will work with at risk juvenile girls. Congratulations again, LaTeishia and best of luck to you in your future endeavours.

Detroit's own Melissa Butler is a woman on the rise. As a graduate of Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University, graduating with a degree in business, she took her talents to Wall Street shortly after graduation. While in New York, she envisioned her own make up line that praises black women and their diverse skin. Thus, The Lip Bar was born. Working for years to get her company to take off, she decided to propose her idea on the hit show, Shark Tank. They did not like the idea and turned her away. However, she proved persistence is key by launching her own website and becoming recognized by Target. She is now selling her makeup online at Target.com. She is definitely an inspiration to young black women. Check her out!
